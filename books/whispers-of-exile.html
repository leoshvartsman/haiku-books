Whispers Of Exile

Ash Green
141 haiku
Download PDF Download EPUB
In the quiet moments, I've often found myself lost in thought, wandering through city streets and countryside landscapes that hold both comfort and disquiet. It's a feeling of being suspended between places, like a leaf caught on an autumn breeze, unable to settle into one particular spot. This sense of displacement has been my constant companion for years, as I've navigated the complexities of identity, memory, and belonging. As I reflect on these experiences, I'm reminded of the whispered stories that have shaped me – the voices of family members, friends, and strangers who have taught me to see the world in new ways. Their words, like wisps of smoke on a winter's night, have seeped into my skin, influencing the way I perceive reality. And so, this collection of haikus is an attempt to capture these ephemeral whispers, to hold them close and examine their resonance within me. In Whispers of Exile, you'll find fragments of my own journey – moments of introspection amidst London's grey haze, Melbourne's golden light, Japan's tranquil seasons, and the in-between places that defy easy categorization. These haikus are not just reflections on displacement; they're also a search for home, both within myself and in the landscapes that surround me.
