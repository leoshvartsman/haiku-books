Mosaic Of Exile

Morgan Kelly
196 haiku
Download PDF Download EPUB
As I sit with these haiku, I'm reminded of the disparate threads that have woven my life together – the emerald hills of Ireland where my story began, the chaotic streets of Bogotá that shook me awake, and the gentle English countryside that brought solace. Each place has left its mark on me, inscribing itself on my heart like a poet's brushstrokes on a canvas. These haiku are fragments of my own exile – not just physical, but also linguistic and cultural. The languages I've spoken, the places I've called home, have shaped me into someone new with each move. In these brief poems, I try to capture the essence of each experience: the grey skies that felt like a perpetual raincloud over Dublin, the vibrant chaos that enveloped me in Bogotá, and the gentle hues that wrapped around me in Norwich. This collection is more than just a journey through places – it's an exploration of the languages that have become part of my soul. Language Bridges are the pathways that connect me to the world beyond myself, while Exile's Fragmented Self reflects the ongoing search for identity and belonging. Mosaic's Endless Beginning hints at the infinite possibilities that arise from these disparate threads.
