Red Earth, Snowflakes

Casey Walker
144 haiku
Download PDF Download EPUB
As I wander through the deserts of Outback Australia, I find myself drawn to the silence that lies at their heart. The red earth stretches out before me, a vast and unforgiving landscape that has become my home. It's here, among the spinifex grass and whispering sand dunes, that I've come to understand the language of stillness. But this collection is not just about the Outback; it's also about the journeys that have brought me to these places – rural New Zealand's rolling hills and sparkling rivers, Tokyo's neon-lit streets and tranquil gardens. In each new landscape, I've sought to listen more deeply to the world around me, to capture the ways in which culture and nature intersect. In Red Earth, Snowflakes, you'll find haiku that weave together fragments of my life – moments of beauty and hardship, of wonder and disorientation. Each section is a chapter in this story, a thread in the tapestry of experiences that have shaped me into who I am today.
