Beneath The Banyan's Shade

Parker Singh
185 haiku
Download PDF Download EPUB
As I sit beneath the sprawling branches of a banyan tree, its gnarled limbs twisted with age, I am reminded of the countless places and moments that have shaped my life. The scent of wet earth and green leaves transports me back to Nepal's misty valleys, where I first began to see the world through new eyes. In Thailand's vibrant streets, the cacophony of sounds and colors overwhelmed me, yet also revealed a beauty both fierce and tender. As an artist, I've always been drawn to the intersection of nature, culture, and the human experience. In this collection, I invite you to join me on a journey through some of my most treasured memories: waking in Kathmandu's dawn light, losing myself in Bangkok's sensory delights, walking Ireland's emerald landscapes, and reflecting on the rhythms and cycles that govern our lives. In these haiku, I've tried to distill the essence of experience into moments of stillness and wonder. Some are born from joy, others from sorrow or regret – but all share a thread of gratitude for the time we have, and the world's beauty that surrounds us.
