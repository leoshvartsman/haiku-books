Fading Light, Istanbul

Robin Andersson
144 haiku
Download PDF Download EPUB
As I wandered through the crumbling streets of Istanbul, I began to notice the way light danced across worn stones and weathered tiles. The whispers of forgotten empires seemed to seep from every corner, their secrets shared only with those willing to listen. It was here that I started to weave together threads of mortality, of aging, and the human experience. The collection, Fading Light, Istanbul, is a personal exploration of the fleeting nature of life. Through haiku, I attempt to capture the essence of various landscapes – from the humid mornings in Charlottesville to the scorching afternoons in Tel Aviv – where every sense is heightened, and the world feels alive. The passage of time becomes palpable, a gentle reminder that each moment is precious. In these pages, I invite you to join me on this journey through the ever-changing tapestry of life. From the whispers of Istanbul's ancient stones to the rustling leaves of Busan's autumn breeze, let us wander together, lost in the beauty and fragility of our existence.
