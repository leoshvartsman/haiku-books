Blessings In Exile

River Singh
181 haiku
Download PDF Download EPUB
In the stillness of my Casablanca home, I find myself drawn to memories of far-off places and people who have shaped me into the person I am today. My childhood in Addis Ababa, with its vibrant markets and ancient traditions, laid the groundwork for a journey that would take me from the concrete canyons of New York to the somber beauty of Hiroshima's hills. As I reflect on these disparate landscapes, I realize that they have all contributed to a deeper understanding of myself – and of the world. In each place, I found moments of transcendence: Ethiopian sunrises that awakened my spirit; city skies in New York that stretched me towards possibility; the river's gentle flow in Hiroshima that reminded me of life's cycles. This haiku collection, "Blessings in Exile", is a weaving together of these threads from different continents and cultures. Each poem distills a moment of connection – to nature, to loved ones, to my own sense of identity. I hope these words will offer solace and beauty to those who read them, and perhaps even illuminate the blessings that can be found in our experiences of displacement and transformation.
