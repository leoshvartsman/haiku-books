Folded Maps

Sam Lewis
191 haiku
Download PDF Download EPUB
As I sit with a cup of tea in hand, surrounded by fragments of my past, I am reminded of the maps that have guided me through life's journey. Folds and creases, like the lines on a worn atlas, tell stories of where I've been, who I've met, and what I've seen. In these haikus, I attempt to capture the essence of moments both grand and intimate, seeking to distill the beauty from the complexities. Folded Maps is a collection born of the intersections between place and memory. The Scottish lochs that whisper secrets in my ear, the Oxford streets where love and heartache entwined, the Taipei alleys that still whisper their tales – each landscape has left an indelible mark on me. I invite you to join me on this poetic journey, where the boundaries between reality and dream blur like the contours of a map. In these pages, you will find the echoes of my footsteps, etched into haikus that weave together disparate threads of experience. These poems are not meant to be destinations, but rather gateways to the memories they evoke. I hope that as you read, you too will discover the maps hidden within your own life – and that the contours of mine might somehow resonate with yours.
