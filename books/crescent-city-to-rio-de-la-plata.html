Crescent City To Rio De La Plata

Logan Jackson
156 haiku
Download PDF Download EPUB
As I sit in my small apartment in New Orleans, surrounded by the whispers of jazz and the scent of beignets, I find myself wandering to distant shores. Memories of Mardi Gras parades and lazy summer afternoons on the Mississippi merge with visions of Montevideo's tranquil coastline and the rhythmic hum of its plazas. The fluidity of life is a river that flows both ways, carrying me back to where I began. This collection, Crescent City to Rio de la Plata, is a journey through the landscapes of my own heart. Haiku by haiku, it navigates the intersections of memory and place, of self and others. Like the ebb and flow of the ocean tides, these poems move between the vibrancy of New Orleans and the serenity of Uruguay. In the following pages, you'll find echoes of jazz in the streets of Montevideo, flamenco rhythms dancing on the winds of the River Plate, and whispers of Spanish moss on the tranquil shores of Myrtle Grove. These haiku are a love letter to two cities that have become home, and an exploration of the fluid boundaries between memory, place, and self.
