Fleeting Horizons

Sol Gómez
155 haiku
Download PDF Download EPUB
As I sit in my study, surrounded by the faint scent of jasmine and the soft glow of twilight, I find myself lost in the horizons that have shaped me. Memories of Madrid's bustling streets, California's golden skies, and the serene bend of a river I once called home all swirl together, a kaleidoscope of moments suspended between past and present. In this collection, Fleeting Horizons, I invite you to walk alongside me through these fragments of my life. Each haiku is a snapshot, a whispered secret, or a tender observation – a glimpse into the world as I've seen it. Nature's rhythms become intertwined with human experiences, revealing the intricate web that binds us all. These pages hold the essence of my wanderings and stayings, where moments of beauty have seeped into my soul like the morning dew on a summer leaf. As you turn through these haiku, I hope you'll discover not only my own vision but also the fleeting horizons that connect us all – the spaces where the boundaries between self and world blur, and the universe reveals its depths to those who listen with an open heart.
