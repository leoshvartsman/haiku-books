Turbulent Leaves

As I sit with pen in hand, surrounded by scattered drafts and dog-eared pages, I'm reminded of the winding paths that led me to these haiku. Turbulent Leaves is a collection born from my own wanderlust – a fusion of memories gathered from city streets, landscapes, and moments that have forever etched themselves into my heart. In this book, you'll find echoes of Miami's fiery sunsets, Tokyo's relentless rain, Lyon's misty mornings, and Hanoi's vibrant blooms. These fragments, distilled from years of travel and reflection, seek to capture the essence of life's rhythms – its ebb and flow, its beauty and turmoil. As I wrote these haiku, I sought not only to document my experiences but also to distill their lessons: that impermanence is a gift, that transformation lies in the everyday, and that even amidst chaos, there is always beauty waiting to be unearthed. In Turbulent Leaves, I invite you to join me on this journey – to breathe in the scent of blooming flowers, to feel the rain-soaked streets beneath your feet, and to listen for the whispers that come at dawn and dusk. May these haiku transport you to places both familiar and unknown, reminding you that even amidst the turbulence of life, there is always a quiet stillness waiting to be discovered.
