Green Mountain summer

fireflies write their brief poems

in disappearing ink

Seminar table

scarred with decades of debate,

coffee rings like trees

Cat watches snowflakes

through glass with predator eyes—

winter's moving prey

Seagull drops clam shell

on the parking lot pavement,

breakfast earned through wit

The church bell tower

leans slightly left after last

winter's ice storm weight

Alpaca wool scarf

still carries Andean wind

through Maine's bitter cold
