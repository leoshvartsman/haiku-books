Philadelphia snow

melts into storm drains carrying

childhood toward the sea

Soft pretzel steam rises

from South Street's crowded corners—

salt crystals catch light

Dhaka street food smoke

rises from oil drums cut in

half and filled with fire

Flight path over home—

city lights spell no language

I recognize now

Street cricket match stops

for funeral procession

then resumes mid-pitch

Flight tower clearance

crackles through my headphones while

kangaroos graze below
