Borderless Skies

Sky Novak
145 haiku
Download PDF Download EPUB
As I sit here, surrounded by the fragmented memories of a life lived between borders, I am reminded that it is in these moments of transition that we find our truest selves. The beauty of impermanence, with its gentle touch and whispered promises, has been my constant companion on this journey. In these haikus, I have tried to capture the essence of a life in motion – the way the wind whispers secrets in canal-side gardens, the fog rolls in like a blanket to shroud the city streets, and the Turkish sunlight warms the heart. Each poem is a piece of a larger tapestry, woven from threads of love, loss, and longing. As I look back on these verses, I am struck by the realization that borders – whether physical or emotional – are merely a starting point for our stories. It is in the spaces between, where cultures blend and languages merge, that we find the true language of the heart. This collection is my attempt to listen to that language, to give voice to the fragments of a life lived on the threshold.
