River Of Memories

Sacha Michel
155 haiku
Download PDF Download EPUB
As I wander through the seasons, memories accumulate like fallen leaves on a forest floor. Fragments of experience, emotions, and encounters intertwine with the natural world, forming an ever-changing tapestry of impressions. In these haiku, I attempt to capture the essence of moments that have left an indelible mark on my soul. Traveling through cities and landscapes has become a form of spiritual pilgrimage for me – a way to connect with the mysteries that lie beyond human constructs. I find solace in the rhythms of nature: the gentle lapping of waves against the shore, the soft chirping of birds at dawn, or the majestic sweep of mountains under starry skies. In this collection, "River of Memories," I invite you to join me on a journey through various corners of the world – Lyon's morning mist, Tokyo's neon dreams, Montreal's autumn breeze, and Dhaka's urban jungle. Here, cultures intersect, stories unfold, and whispers of the past carry us forward into the unknown.
