Beneath Aurora Skies

Casey White
166 haiku
Download PDF Download EPUB
As I look out upon the ever-changing cityscapes that have been my home, I'm struck by the way nature and human life intersect. The urban jungle, with its steel and concrete façades, is where I've grown most deeply – where childhood memories of Denver's snow-kissed peaks gave way to the frenetic rhythms of Hanoi's streets, and now, in Reykjavik, the land of fire and ice has claimed my heart. This collection, Beneath Aurora Skies, represents a selection of haiku moments from across these various cities, each one a glimpse into the world I've observed and inhabited. From the stillness of winter mountains to the vibrant chaos of street markets, I invite you to join me on this journey through the intersection of nature and human life. In these brief poems, I aim to distill the essence of my surroundings – the way light filters through skyscrapers, or the scent of rain on pavement – into a world that's both intimate and universal.
