Whispers In Exile

Haruki Saito
125 haiku
Download PDF Download EPUB
As I sit amidst the Eternal City's winding streets and ancient stones, I am reminded of the journeys that have brought me to this place – the whispers of my childhood in Kyoto's temples, the lessons learned in Ann Arbor's winter snows, the laughter shared with friends in Nairobi's vibrant markets. These experiences have woven a tapestry of displacement and belonging, one that I hope to share through these haiku. In Whispers in Exile, I invite you to join me on this path of self-discovery and connection. Through the seasons, cities, and cultures that have shaped my life, I offer glimpses into the human experience – moments of joy, sorrow, wonder, and resilience. These poems are not just reflections of my own story but also a testament to the universal language of poetry. As you wander through these pages, I hope you'll find echoes of your own experiences in the whispers of Sakura Snowflakes, Kuumba Kinship, Fading Light, Ginkgo Leaves, Tanka Traverses, and Luminous Threads. May our shared humanity illuminate the paths we've walked, and may the quiet moments between words speak louder than any language.
