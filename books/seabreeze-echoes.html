Seabreeze Echoes

Jun Jang
197 haiku
Download PDF Download EPUB
As I sit on my porch, surrounded by the whispers of sea and sky, I am reminded of the winds that carried me to this moment. The echoes of childhood summers in Busan still linger, where the scent of saltwater and sesame seeds wafted through our small neighborhood streets. But it's not just memories of home that have shaped these haiku – it's the countless hours spent walking the dunes of Provincetown, gazing up at Hong Kong's glittering skyscrapers, or strolling along Santiago's tranquil riverside paths. In Seabreeze Echoes, I invite you to join me on a journey through the intersections of East and West. These haiku are an attempt to capture the rhythms that bind us – the tides that rise and fall with the sun, the seasons that ebb and flow with our lives. They're a reflection of my own wanderings, as well as those of countless others who have made their homes in these diverse cities. As I gathered these poems, I began to notice how the natural world spoke a common language across cultures – a language of light and shadow, wind and water. In this collection, I hope you'll find moments of connection, echoes that resonate within your own experience of the world.
