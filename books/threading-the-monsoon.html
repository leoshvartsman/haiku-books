Sample Haiku

Sparrow builds her nest

with city debris and hope

architecture of instinct

Child's paper airplane

Soars above the slum's tin roofs—

Dreams need little fuel

Mother braids daughter's hair

black rope thick as temple cord—

generations weave

Barber's razor gleams

On cheek lined with eighty years

Grooming for the grave

Spice merchant measures

Turmeric in brass scales old

Golden dust of time

Temple cat stretches

across warm marble threshold

ignoring pilgrims
