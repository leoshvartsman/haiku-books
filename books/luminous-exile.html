Luminous Exile

Morgan Gomes
175 haiku
Download PDF Download EPUB
As I sit with pen in hand, reflecting on the journeys that have brought me to this moment, I am reminded of the threads that weave through my life – languages, landscapes, and cultures that have shaped me into the poet I am today. Growing up in Brazil, surrounded by the vibrant rhythms of Carnival, and later discovering the tranquility of Japanese gardens in Tokyo, each place has left an indelible mark on my soul. This collection, Luminous Exile, is a testament to those formative experiences that have merged and transformed within me over time. Haiku, with its concise yet evocative beauty, became the perfect vessel for me to capture the essence of these encounters. As I navigated the intricacies of language and culture, I found solace in this ancient poetic form – a bridge between worlds, where meaning is distilled into a few, exquisite words. In Luminous Exile, you will find fragments of my life, suspended like petals on the wind. These haiku are not just reflections of places, but also of the people and moments that have made me who I am. As you wander through these pages, I invite you to join me in the intersections where cultures meet, languages blend, and memories unfold.
