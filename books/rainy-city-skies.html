Rainy City Skies

Taylor Anderson
167 haiku
Download PDF Download EPUB
As I wander through the rain-slick streets, Philadelphia's ancient stones whisper secrets in my ear. The city's rhythms – a jazz saxophone's mournful wail, the thrum of the El train – are woven into the fabric of these poems, alongside the vibrant pulse of Rio de Janeiro and the ethereal glow of Reykjavik's night sky. In "Rainy City Skies," I invite you to join me on a poetic journey through urban landscapes that have shaped my life. These haikus distill moments of wonder, each one a drop in the vast ocean of memories that make up our cities' collective past. I've chased rainbows and danced under storm clouds, and in these poems, I've tried to capture the ephemeral beauty of city life. As you turn these pages, I hope you'll catch glimpses of yourself reflected in the puddles and windows of these rainy city streets. Let the poetry carry you away on a tide of wonder, as we explore together the hidden corners and secret landscapes that make our cities so richly human.
