Terra Verde

Quinn Jones
160 haiku
Download PDF Download EPUB
As I sit amidst the lush greens of my backyard, surrounded by the vibrant blooms and sounds of nature, I am reminded of the many landscapes that have shaped me into the person I am today. Terra Verde is a collection of haikus born from the confluence of memories, experiences, and observations that have traversed continents and seasons. From the sun-kissed streets of my childhood in Brisbane to the concrete canyons of New York City, each poem is a snapshot of a moment, a mood, or an emotion that has lingered long after the experience has passed. As I reflect on the places and people that have touched my life, I am struck by the threads that weave our stories together – the smells of distant markets, the taste of unfamiliar foods, the rhythm of foreign languages. These haikus are not just about the landscapes themselves but also about the memories, emotions, and sensations that arise when we engage with them. I invite you to join me on this journey through Terra Verde, where the lines between language and culture blur, and the beauty of the everyday is revealed. Through these haikus, I hope to convey the depth and richness of human experience, which knows no borders or boundaries. In the words of a friend who read early drafts, "These poems are like sips of tea on a winter morning – small, yet expansive, each one transporting you to another world."
