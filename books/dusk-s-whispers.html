Dusk'S Whispers

Sage Sato
195 haiku
Download PDF Download EPUB
As I sit on my windowsill, watching dusk settle over the city, I'm reminded that memories are like whispers – soft and fleeting, yet they stay with us long after the moment has passed. My haiku collection, Dusk's Whispers, is an invitation to join me on a journey through the landscapes of my own past. I've woven together moments from different chapters of my life: Lagos nights spent swaying to the rhythm of drums; London days filled with the whispers of strangers in crowded streets; Toronto evenings where lake breezes carried the scent of distant forests. Each haiku is a snapshot, a distillation of what I saw, felt, and breathed in those moments. In Dusk's Whispers, I've tried to capture not just the external world, but also the inner landscapes that unfold when day meets night. These poems are an attempt to listen to the whispers of my own heart – to the memories that have shaped me, and to the dreams that still linger like starlight on a summer evening.
