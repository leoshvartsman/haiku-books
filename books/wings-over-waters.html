Wings Over Waters

Sky Novak
142 haiku
Download PDF Download EPUB
As I look back on my journey from one continent to another, I am reminded of the words that carried me across borders: poetry, language, and the memories of waters left behind. This collection, Wings Over Waters, is a reflection on my experiences as an immigrant, navigating multiple cultures and landscapes. From childhood explorations along Amsterdam's canals to the bustling streets of Istanbul, each place has left its mark on me. I wrote these haiku as a way to hold onto those moments, to remember the feel of wind in my hair, the taste of new foods, and the scent of unfamiliar spices. They are whispers from one world to another. In these pages, you will find fragments of stories told in three lines – glimpses into what it means to be caught between two worlds, searching for a place to call home. The haiku here are not just poetry, but memories frozen in time, reminders of the journey that has led me to where I stand today.
