Rainy Season Reflections

Jamie Martinez
141 haiku
Download PDF Download EPUB
In the ebb and flow of life's seasons, I've found myself drawn to the quiet moments – a morning rain in Miami, the first snowfall in Vermont, the sweltering heat of a Bangkok afternoon. As the years have passed, I've come to realize that it's not just the weather that changes, but our very selves. In these haiku, I've tried to capture the essence of those moments – moments of transition, of letting go, and of embracing the present. From the sultry nights of Miami to the rugged beauty of Vermont's winter landscape, each poem is a reflection on the human experience: born, lived, loved, lost. This collection, Rainy Season Reflections, is an invitation to slow down, to pause amidst the chaos of daily life and breathe in the beauty that surrounds us. In these quiet moments, we find our own stories – tales of love, loss, growth, and transformation. I hope you'll join me on this journey through time, as we navigate the intersections of aging, mortality, and the passing of seasons.
