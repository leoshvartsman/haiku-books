Monsoon Whispers

Arnav Singh
169 haiku
Download PDF Download EPUB
As I sit in my Hanoi apartment, surrounded by the vibrant chaos of this city, I'm transported back to the monsoon rains that swept through Mumbai's streets when I was a child. The scent of wet earth and ozone still lingers on my skin, reminding me of the sounds, sights, and textures that have stayed with me throughout my journeys. This collection, Monsoon Whispers, is an attempt to capture those fragments – the way light danced on a Toronto sidewalk in autumn, the eerie silence of Rome's night markets, or the cacophony of Hanoi's streets at dawn. Each haiku is a snapshot of a moment, frozen in time like a Polaroid photograph. I've woven these images together with the rhythms that have accompanied me – the jazz beats that pulsed through my veins in each city, and the quiet moments when life slowed down. In these pages, you'll find fragments of myself – memories, experiences, and emotions distilled into haiku. It's a personal journey, one that I invite you to join me on as we navigate the intersections of place, culture, and time.
