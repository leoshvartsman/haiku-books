Fractured Luminance

Parker Novak
178 haiku
Download PDF Download EPUB
As I sit with these haikus, I'm reminded of the fractured light that has shaped my life. A life spent chasing shadows and moments of beauty in unlikely places – Singapore's humid monsoons, Vienna's Art Nouveau courtyards, Santiago's vibrant streets. Each location a prism that refracted the world into something new, yet familiar. These haikus are fragments of those experiences, gathered like seashells on a beach. I've tried to capture the essence of what made each place special – the scent of rain-soaked pavements in Singapore, the melancholy of a Vienna winter evening, the joyous chaos of Santiago's markets. They're not memoirs, exactly, but rather echoes of a life lived in transit. I hope that as you read these haikus, you'll find yourself transported to those places, and perhaps even discover something of yourself in their fractured luminance.
