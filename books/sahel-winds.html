Sahel Winds

Casey Chen
132 haiku
Download PDF Download EPUB
As I wandered through the Sahel's vast expanse, I felt the whispers of distant cultures carried on the wind. The rhythms of Africa, the quiet of New England, and the vibrant pulse of Europe all merged in my mind like the threads of a rich tapestry. In these haiku, I've tried to capture the essence of those moments when time and space converge. From the streets of Addis Ababa to the nights of Ann Arbor, from the Bosphorus' melancholy shores to Boston's springtime blooms, each section is a slice of life, distilled into three brief lines. Yet in their simplicity lies a world of depth and complexity – a reflection of our shared human experience. For me, the Sahel Winds collection represents a journey of discovery, not just of far-off lands but also of my own place within them. These haiku are invitations to step into that space where cultures intersect, where moments pass into eternity, and where the beauty of the world can be found in its most fleeting forms.
