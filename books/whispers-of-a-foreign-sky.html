Whispers Of A Foreign Sky

Ming Xu
171 haiku
Download PDF Download EPUB
As I reflect on the threads that weave together these haikus, I am reminded of the whispered conversations with strangers in foreign cities, and the familiar streets of my childhood. The world, it seems, is a tapestry of intersecting stories, each one imbuing the mundane with an otherworldly essence. In this collection, Whispers of a Foreign Sky, I invite you to join me on a poetic journey through the cities that have shaped me – Shanghai, New York, Chicago, and Berlin. Each city is a portal to a unique realm, where the ordinary becomes extraordinary, and the past converges with the present. My haikus are an attempt to capture the essence of these places, to distill the complexities of human experience into moments of quiet beauty. This collection is not just about cities; it's also a map of my own personal history – of memories forged in childhood, of love and loss, of identity and belonging. I hope that as you read through Whispers of a Foreign Sky, you will discover not only the stories of these places but also the echoes of your own experiences reflected within them.
