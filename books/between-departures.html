Window box geraniums

red against gray concrete wall

small rebellions bloom

cracked from many refugee hands

seeking language keys

over satellite dishes—

old voices, new ears

storks' nests built from twigs and wire

birds know no borders

graffiti in three languages

tells stories of home

lamb döner spinning slowly

hunger has no passport
