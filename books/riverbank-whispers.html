Riverbank Whispers

Morgan Andersson
174 haiku
Download PDF Download EPUB
I recall mornings in Zurich's streets, where fog swirled around buildings like wisps of smoke, shrouding the world in a soft grey mist. The quiet was almost palpable, broken only by the gentle hum of espresso machines and the murmur of pedestrians hurrying to work. In these moments, I felt most connected to the city's rhythm. As a traveler, I've been drawn to places where the past and present converge – Kyoto's temples, Florence's piazzas, Busan's seaside boulevards. Each location has its own unique cadence, yet they're also bound together by an unseen thread: the struggles of those who call them home. In my haiku collection, I've attempted to capture these threads, weaving them into a tapestry that's both intimate and public. The Riverbank Whispers series is my attempt to distill the essence of these places, their people, and the moments they share with me. Each haiku is a snapshot of life as it unfolds – fragmented, imperfect, yet radiant in its own way. I invite you to join me on this journey, to walk alongside me through the streets, alleys, and rivers that have become a part of my soul.
