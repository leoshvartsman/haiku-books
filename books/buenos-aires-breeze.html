Buenos Aires Breeze

Valentín García
184 haiku
Download PDF Download EPUB
As I sit here in Paris, surrounded by the soft glow of streetlights and the gentle hum of the city, my mind drifts back to childhood summers spent chasing the Buenos Aires breeze through streets lined with cypress trees. Memories of laughter and long afternoons linger, infused with the scent of freshly cut grass and the sound of distant tangos. It's as if those early days are woven into the very fabric of my being, waiting to be unraveled. Over time, life led me down divergent paths: Dubai's scorching dunes and vast expanse of sand; Paris' winding Seine and artistic whispers; even a season in Tennessee's twilight-hued hills. Yet, amidst these disparate landscapes, I've found an unshakeable connection to the land – its rhythms, its moods, its sacred moments. This collection, Buenos Aires Breeze, seeks to capture that elusive thread, the one that weaves together the spiritual and the natural. In the following pages, you'll find fragments of my journey: River Plate Skies' melancholic sighs; Desert Blooms' fierce beauty; Seine River Musings' introspective musings. Each section a portal to another time and place, yet all connected by an unseen current that whispers secrets only known to those who wander between worlds.
