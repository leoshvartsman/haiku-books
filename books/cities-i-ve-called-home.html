Cities I've Called Home

Sage Singh
222 haiku
Download PDF Download EPUB
As I sit here, surrounded by scraps of paper and scattered thoughts, I'm reminded of all the places that have become a part of me. Cities I've called home, each one etched in my memory like the lines on a well-loved book. Lima's golden light still warms my skin, Sydney's morning breeze stirs my soul, Bangkok's raindrops whisper secrets to my ears. This collection is a map of sorts – a map not of streets and landmarks, but of the moments that made me who I am today. Memories of childhood adventures in Peru blend with the excitement of new beginnings in Australia and Thailand. And now, as I navigate the vibrant heartbeat of Tel Aviv, I realize that home is where the heart finds its rhythm. In these haikus, you'll find fragments of my story – snippets of joy, longing, and wonder. They're a reflection of the landscapes that have shaped me, but also of the moments in between: the silences, the pauses, the whispers of remembered days.
