Whispers Of The City

Lou Bernard
194 haiku
Download PDF Download EPUB
As I wander through the city streets, I find myself entranced by the symphony of sounds and sights that surround me. The way a streetlamp's glow casts shadows on wet pavement, the soft chatter of passersby in a café, the distant hum of the métro – these are the whispers of the city, echoing secrets only known to those who listen closely. In this collection, I've sought to capture the essence of urban landscapes that have captivated me: Paris's storied beauty, Munich's grandeur, Oxford's hallowed halls, and Tokyo's futuristic pulse. Each city has left its mark on my imagination, and in these haikus, I aim to distill the unique character of each place into a few simple words. As I wrote, I found myself drawn to the tension between the natural world – with all its beauty and decay – and the human-made landscape that surrounds us. The city is at once sterile and vibrant, loud and quiet; it's this paradox that fascinates me. In Whispers of the City, I invite you to join me on a poetic journey through these contrasts, to find solace in the rhythms of the metropolis.
