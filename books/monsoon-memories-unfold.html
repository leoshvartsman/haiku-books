Monsoon Memories Unfold

Parker Andersson
209 haiku
Download PDF Download EPUB
As I sit here, surrounded by the whispers of yesterday's rains, I am reminded that life is a tapestry woven from countless moments, each one a thread in the rich fabric of experience. My mind wanders back to Ho Chi Minh City, where the scent of street food mingled with the damp earth after every monsoon storm. It was there that I first learned to let go, to surrender to the rhythm of the seasons. Mumbai's cacophony and Shanghai's quiet grandeur also left their mark on me, each city a chapter in my life's story. As I grew older, I found solace in the stillness of morning dew, the way it glistened like tears on the petals of forgotten flowers. And yet, with age comes the realization that even in the most serene moments, the past lingers, its whispers carried on the wind. In these haikus, I have attempted to distill the essence of my life's journey – a journey marked by contrasts and convergences. Here, you will find fragments of memories from cities that were once foreign, now etched in my soul like the scars on a well-loved book. This collection is an invitation to walk with me through the twilight of my years, where shadows dance and the past comes alive in every drop of rain.
