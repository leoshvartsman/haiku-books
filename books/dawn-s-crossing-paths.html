Dawn's Crossing Paths

Sora Nakamura
170 haiku
Download PDF Download EPUB
As I wandered through Japan's misty mornings, New Zealand's rugged coastlines, and China's ancient streets, I found myself lost in the fleeting moments of dawn's arrival. The gentle light that creeps over mountains and oceans, casting shadows on familiar faces and places, sparked something within me. It was as if the world was awakening from a slumber, and with it, my own senses. These haikus are a record of my journey, a mapping of the intersections where dawn's radiance touched the lives of strangers, friends, and loved ones. They capture the serendipitous moments when light, place, and person converge, illuminating the intricate web of connections that binds us to each other and to the land. Within these pages, I invite you to walk with me through Kyoto's whispering temples, Tokyo's neon-lit alleys, Auckland's windswept shores, Beijing's bustling streets, and the serene stillness of waters' edges. May the dawn's crossings guide us into a deeper appreciation for the beauty that arises at the intersections of life.
