The How of the Why

Lenny Black
370 haiku
Download PDF Download EPUB
Lenny Black is not a poet. He will tell you this himself, possibly while standing at the kitchen counter, scrolling past something he should read, in a house that is comfortable in all the ways that do not quite add up to content. What he is, or was, is a man with a lawn and a browser history and a feeling he could not name—a feeling that seemed too small for therapy and too large for small talk, the kind that accumulates in the pauses between ordinary things. This book is the record of what happened when he decided, half out of boredom and half out of something he would not yet call desperation, to hand that feeling to a machine and ask it to speak.
