Fading Light In Foreign Cities

Ming Xu
166 haiku
Download PDF Download EPUB
As I wandered through foreign cities, I found myself lost not only in unfamiliar streets but also in the labyrinth of my own heart. The urban landscapes, with their steel and stone facades, seemed to mirror the fragmented nature of my experiences – a patchwork quilt of memories, emotions, and longing. In Fading Light in Foreign Cities, I invite you to join me on a poetic journey through the cities that have shaped me: Shanghai's humid summer afternoons, New York's bright lights and lonely nights, Chicago's quiet bookstores, Berlin's crumbling walls, and the invisible borders that crisscross my soul. Each city has left its mark on me, etching stories of love, loss, and resilience onto my skin like a map. These haikus are fragments of myself, suspended between cultures and languages, yet connected by an unspoken thread of human experience. As you read, I hope to transport you not only to the cities but also into the depths of our shared humanity – where the ache of longing and the joy of connection converge in a delicate dance of light and shadow.
