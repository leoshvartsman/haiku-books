Sample Haiku

fog lifts from the Vltava—

grandmother's flour-dusted wrist

catches the first light

arrival card, pen,

the box for purpose of visit:

I write: to remain

dogwood against grey

neither the white nor the grey

gives way to the other

my pen runs dry once

each fiscal year—I mark it,

replace it, move on

incense smoke bends left

then straightens—the audit finds

what bending concealed

monsoon fills the drains—

the sound beneath the city

waking, running out
