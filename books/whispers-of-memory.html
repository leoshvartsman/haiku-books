Whispers Of Memory

Harper García
143 haiku
Download PDF Download EPUB
As I sit with these haiku, scattered like leaves on a forgotten path, I'm reminded of the countless moments that have shaped me – moments that whisper their secrets in the stillness. Memories that refuse to be tamed, they seep into my writing like water into the earth. In these pages, you'll find fragments of my life, intertwined with the landscapes that have cradled them. Taipei's dawn mist, a Norwegian winter's darkness, and the urban canyons of home – each place has etched its own story upon me. I've tried to capture the essence of these experiences in haiku, those delicate snapshots of time. This collection is not just about memories; it's also an exploration of our relationship with the world around us. As we navigate the intersections of identity and environmental consciousness, I'm drawn to the quiet moments that reveal our place within the larger tapestry.
