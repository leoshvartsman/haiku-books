Fractured Light

Riley García
164 haiku
Download PDF Download EPUB
In the fragmented light of our times, I've sought to capture the beauty and the pain of a life lived in the margins. As an artist and a witness, I've navigated cities that are at once beautiful and brutal – where the poetry of existence is often tempered by the politics of inequality. These haiku are born from the spaces between: Stockholm's snow-glazed streets, Vienna's twilight, and the quiet hours spent amidst books in a dusty shop. Each moment, each image, each emotion is a thread in the tapestry I've tried to weave – a tapestry that reflects the dissonance and the beauty of our world. For me, haiku has always been a form of resistance – a way to hold onto the fragments of experience, to transform them into something more than just memory. In these pages, you'll find moments of tenderness and rage, of loss and love. They are a reflection of my own fractured light, and I hope they may illuminate your own path in some small way.
