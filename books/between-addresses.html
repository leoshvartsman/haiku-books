Between Addresses

Harper Smith

Download PDF Download EPUB
Harper Smith spent four decades calculating the cost of uncertainty for other people. As an insurance agent, he learned to read a life the way a surveyor reads land — for elevation, for fault lines, for what might give way and when. He worked in cities that did not share a climate or a language, paid rent in currencies that no longer exist, and trained himself to convert everything into numbers that could be compared, transferred, filed. He was, by professional necessity, a man who believed that risk could be made legible. This book is his reckoning with what that belief left out.
