Dawn's Mosaic

Quinn Smith
178 haiku
Download PDF Download EPUB
As I sit amidst the dawn's quiet whispers, I am reminded that life is a tapestry woven from diverse threads. In this collection, Dawn's Mosaic, I invite you to step into my world, where the sights, sounds, and scents of different landscapes converge. Growing up in Auckland's lush green hills, I learned to appreciate the subtleties of nature. Later, Melbourne's academic rigors honed my focus and creativity. But it was India's rural landscapes that revealed to me the beauty of impermanence and the interconnectedness of all things. And now, as I navigate Tel Aviv's vibrant cityscape, I find myself at an intersection of cultures, languages, and experiences. In these haiku, I attempt to capture the essence of my life's journey so far – a mosaic of memories, emotions, and observations. From the intricate patterns of Tā Moko to the pulsating rhythms of Tel Aviv's urban beat, each section weaves its own unique narrative thread into the tapestry. Come, let us walk together through this dawn-lit world, where nature's beauty, human connection, and personal growth entwine like the threads of a richly patterned mosaic.
