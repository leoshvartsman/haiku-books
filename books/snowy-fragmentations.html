Snowy Fragmentations

Ji Seo
147 haiku
Download PDF Download EPUB
As I wander through the fragmented landscapes of my life, I find solace in the stillness of snow-covered hills and the whispers of winter's silence. These haiku are born from the intersections of cultures, climates, and identities that have shaped me: the neon-lit streets of Seoul, where my childhood memories reside; the leafy trees of Toronto, which witnessed my early adult struggles; the rugged coastlines of Norway, where I sought solace in nature's vastness; and the snowy cities of Stockholm, where love and loss intertwined. In these haiku, I attempt to capture the essence of the seasons that have passed through me – the warmth of spring in Seoul, the vibrant hues of autumn in Toronto, the somber gray of winter in Norway, and the delicate promise of spring in Sweden. Each poem is a fragment of my journey, a moment suspended between cultures, climates, and selves. Snowy Fragmentations is more than just a collection of haiku – it's an exploration of the fractured horizons that shape us, and how we navigate the spaces between. I invite you to step into this winter wonderland with me, where words blend with snowflakes, and the boundaries between self and world dissolve.
