Whispers Of Place

River Brown
161 haiku
Download PDF Download EPUB
As I sit here in my Kathmandu home, surrounded by the whispers of place that have shaped me, I am reminded of the winding paths that have brought me to this moment. Memories of childhood mornings spent chasing sunbeams through Taipei's narrow alleys still linger, transporting me back to a time when the world was full of wonder and possibility. As an adult, my travels took me to far-flung corners of the globe – Quito, where I first fell in love with the Inca legacy etched into its ancient stones; Various (a place that defies easy description), which awakened within me a sense of disorientation and awe; and Kathmandu itself, where the labyrinthine streets and sacred temples have become my second home. Through it all, I found myself reaching for words to capture the essence of these places – their textures, scents, sounds, and silences. In Whispers of Place, I offer a collection of haiku that distill the memories and emotions I've accumulated on this journey. Each poem is a snapshot of a moment in time, a fleeting glimpse of the world as it was experienced by my senses. These whispers are for anyone who has ever felt a place seep into their soul, becoming an indelible part of who they are.
