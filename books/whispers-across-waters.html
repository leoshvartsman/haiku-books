Whispers Across Waters

Harper Brown
125 haiku
Download PDF Download EPUB
As I sit by the water's edge, watching the light dance across the waves, I am reminded of the countless journeys that have shaped me. From the rugged coastlines of New Zealand to the city streets of Boston, each place has left its mark on my soul. In these haikus, I've tried to capture the essence of the landscapes that have become a part of me. Some are familiar, like the misty mornings in Wellington where I first felt a sense of belonging. Others are fleeting memories, like the warm glow of Barcelona's light that seemed to promise endless possibilities. And then there are the darker moments – the displacement and uncertainty that can leave one feeling lost and adrift. These haikus are an attempt to weave together these disparate threads, to find meaning in the spaces between. In this collection, I invite you to join me on a journey of self-discovery and exploration. Allow yourself to be swept up in the whispers of the wind, to feel the rough texture of rocky shores beneath your feet. Let these haikus be a reminder that even in displacement, there is always a sense of home – a place where the heart can find peace.
