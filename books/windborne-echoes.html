Windborne Echoes

Taylor Patel
164 haiku
Download PDF Download EPUB
As I sit with these haikus, gathered like leaves on a branch, I'm reminded of the countless moments that have shaped me. The wind whispers secrets to me, and I've tried to capture those echoes in words. This collection is an invitation to step into my journey, to breathe in the scent of eucalyptus and olives, to listen for the jazz rhythms that pulse through every place. In these pages, you'll find fragments of my life: the misty mountains of Cape Town, where I learned to navigate uncertainty; the vibrant streets of New York City, where I discovered my voice; the peaceful woods of Vermont, where I found solace in silence. And between each place, a thread of music weaves its way – a nod to the improvisational spirit that informs this poetry. As I've written these haikus, I've come to see that our experiences are intertwined like the threads of a tapestry. What begins as a whisper in one corner becomes a triumphant shout in another. In Windborne Echoes, I hope you'll find yourself carried by the wind, drawn into the world of these poems, where music and memory blend together in an ever-unfolding dance.
