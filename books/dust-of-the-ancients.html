Dust Of The Ancients

Morgan Kim
143 haiku
Download PDF Download EPUB
As I sit amidst the whispers of dust and memory, I am reminded of the countless days spent chasing sunsets in small-town Canada. The wide open skies of my childhood still echo within me, a symphony of wonder that has followed me across continents. In these haiku, I invite you to join me on a journey through the intersections of past and present. The dusty roads of Johannesburg, the vibrant streets of Ann Arbor, and the ancient alleys of Addis Ababa – each place has left its mark upon my soul. These poems are not just reflections of where I've been, but also of what I've carried with me: the light of a Michigan morning, the whispers of a Saskatchewan prairie wind, and the echoes of memories that refuse to fade. In this collection, "Dust of the Ancients," I hope to transport you to places both familiar and unknown. The haiku are not just poems – they are snapshots of moments in time, each one infused with the scent of dust, memory, and possibility. As you turn these pages, I invite you to breathe deeply, to let the essence of these landscapes settle upon your skin, and to remember that even in the midst of chaos, there is always beauty to be found.
