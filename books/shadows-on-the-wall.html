Shadows On The Wall

Ryan Walsh
196 haiku
Download PDF Download EPUB
In the quiet hours before dawn, when shadows stretch across the walls, I find myself lost in memories of a life lived. As a child, I'd lie awake, listening to my father's stories of distant lands and vibrant cultures. These fragments of experience have stayed with me, weaving together into a tapestry that now informs this collection. I've tried to capture the essence of those early moments – the wonder, the curiosity – in the haikus gathered here. From Dublin's gentle mornings to Casablanca's star-filled nights, each section represents a slice of my journey, infused with the quiet reverence for life and its mysteries that I've come to appreciate. I hope these poems will transport you, as they have me, to far-off places and timeless moments – to African sunsets, American landscapes, Chinese mist, and the whispers of the past. In their silence, I find solace; in their stillness, a sense of connection to something greater than myself.
