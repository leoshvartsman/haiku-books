Whispers From The Winding Water

River Smith
120 haiku
Download PDF Download EPUB
I've woven together fragments of my life into this collection of haiku. Whispers from the Winding Water is a journey through the seasons, and the memories they hold. A snowflake falling in Zurich, a summer sunset on California's coast, a gray Chicago sky – each moment a thread in the tapestry I've tried to create. As I wove these threads together, I found myself drawn back to the winding water that has been my constant companion throughout the years. The way it reflects the light of dawn and dusk, the sounds it whispers as it flows over stones and around bends – all these have left their mark on me, and in turn, on this collection. These haiku are not just poems; they're fragments of myself, caught in the fleeting moments that make up a life. I hope you'll join me on this journey through the landscapes of my experience, where seasons intersect with memories, and language becomes a living, breathing part of the world around us.
