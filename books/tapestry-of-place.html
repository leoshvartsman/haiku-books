Tapestry Of Place

Sage Andersson
163 haiku
Download PDF Download EPUB
As I sit here, surrounded by fragments of my journeys, I'm reminded that every place holds a piece of me. Quito's twilight shadows, Tokyo's neon lights, Coastal Norway's wild landscapes – each has etched its mark on my soul. In this collection, I invite you to step into the tapestry of my experiences, where memories blend with the rhythms of nature and the hum of human connection. These haikus are not just snapshots of places; they're threads that weave together the stories of who I've become. With each breath, I've inhaled the essence of these lands – their beauty, pain, wonder. As I look back, I see how they've informed my own search for meaning, and I'm grateful for the lessons learned along the way. In this collection, you'll find moments of tranquility and chaos, of awe and uncertainty. You'll follow me through city streets and wilderness trails, where the boundaries between self and landscape blur. And in the stillness, perhaps you'll discover a thread that resonates with your own journey – a reminder that we're all part of this intricate tapestry, woven from the same fragile yet resilient fabric of human experience.
