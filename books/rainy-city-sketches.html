Rainy City Sketches

Alex Singh
140 haiku
Download PDF Download EPUB
As I reflect on the years that have passed, I find myself drawn to the memories etched in the weathered pages of my mind. The scent of wet earth after an Addis Ababa monsoon, the Baltimore sunsets that painted the rooftops gold, the misty Quito mornings that whispered secrets of the Andes – each moment a thread in the tapestry of my life. In this collection, I invite you to join me on a journey through Rainy City Sketches. As we wander together, the haiku will unfold like a series of snapshots, capturing the essence of different cities and seasons. We'll walk under the shade of Baltimore's cherry blossom trees, feel the warmth of Quito's sun on our skin, and listen to the rhythms of Montevideo's morning tides. Through these haiku, I aim to distill the sensory landscapes that have shaped me – the way rain sounds on rooftops, the scent of fresh mint in Addis markets, or the taste of a warm Empanada Gallega in Quito. Rainy City Sketches is not just a collection of poems; it's an invitation to slow down and savor the beauty that surrounds us, no matter where we are in the world.
