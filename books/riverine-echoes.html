Riverine Echoes

Ariel Sánchez
133 haiku
Download PDF Download EPUB
As I sit by the river, watching the water's edge where earth and sky meet, I am reminded of the countless moments that have shaped this collection. Riverine Echoes is a map of my wanderings – from the sultry nights of Buenos Aires to the terracotta hills of Tuscany, from the rugged coast of Massachusetts to the verdant plains of India. In these pages, I've tried to capture the essence of the places that have become a part of me. A tango dancer's heartbeat in the moonlight, the scent of wet earth after an Italian storm, the whispers of the dunes on Cape Cod – each experience has left its mark on my soul. I've woven them together with threads of wonder and awe, to create a tapestry that reflects the intricate web of human lives intertwined with the natural world. As I read through these haikus, I'm transported back to those moments of stillness and joy, where the boundaries between self and environment blurred. Riverine Echoes is my attempt to share those echoes – whispers from the river's depths – with you, in the hope that they will resonate within your own heart and soul.
