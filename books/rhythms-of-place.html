Rhythms Of Place

Sage Clark
185 haiku
Download PDF Download EPUB
As I sit amidst the rolling hills of Tuscany, surrounded by cypress trees and golden light, I am reminded of the threads that weave my life together. Memories of childhood mornings in Austin, spent chasing fireflies and watching the dew-kissed grass sparkle like diamonds, still linger in my mind's eye. The scent of eucalyptus and saltwater from Sydney summers beckons me to stroll along the Opera House steps once more. These haiku are snapshots of a life traversed – moments of joy, of heartache, of wonder, and of awe. Each one is a fragment of a larger tapestry, woven from threads of place, time, and emotion. In Parisian evening strolls through Montmartre's winding streets, I found solace in the City of Light's gentle rhythm. The sun-kissed hills of Tuscany now cradle me, yet memories of far-off places remain vivid, like the colors of a dream still lingering on my waking mind. As I share these haiku with you, I invite you to step into the Rhythms of Place that have shaped me – and perhaps discover your own echoes within. The language of seasons whispers secrets in my ear; garden memories bloom like wildflowers in the garden of remembrance. May this collection be a gentle breeze rustling the leaves of your own story, carrying you on a journey through the landscapes of the human heart.
