Exile'S Twilight Bloom

Wen Huang
121 haiku
Download PDF Download EPUB
As I look back on the paths I've wandered, memories bloom like lotus flowers in the twilight of my mind. The cities and landscapes that have been my home for a time – Shanghai's humid nights, Oxford's misty mornings, Delhi's dusty afternoons, and the Australian Outback's star-studded skies – each one has left its indelible mark on me. In this collection, Exile's Twilight Bloom, I've sought to capture the fleeting moments of beauty that have sustained me through the years. The haikus that follow are not just snapshots of place and time; they're also fragments of my own story, woven from the threads of experience and imagination. As I reflect on my journeys, I find that the boundaries between self and landscape begin to blur – and it's in these moments of overlap that poetry is born. In these pages, you'll discover a world of contrasts: light and shadow, silence and sound, stillness and movement. It's a world that I've come to love, one where the rhythm of life is as much about adaptation as it is about belonging. And so, I invite you to join me on this journey through the twilight hours – may we walk together under the same starry skies.
