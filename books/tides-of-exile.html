Tides Of Exile

Cameron Lewis
148 haiku
Download PDF Download EPUB
As I sit with pen in hand, reflecting on the winding path that has led me to this collection, I am met with a sense of wonder and gratitude. Tides of Exile is more than a gathering of haiku – it's a cartography of my soul, mapping the intersections where cultures converge and diverge. From the mist-shrouded mornings of Maine to the vibrant monsoons of Mumbai, each poem is a thread in the tapestry of a life spent navigating the complexities of identity, place, and belonging. Growing up between worlds, I've often found myself caught between two shores, unsure which tide to ride. This experience has been both a source of disorientation and a catalyst for growth, teaching me to listen to the whispers of disparate landscapes and cultures. In these haiku, I attempt to capture the essence of this liminal existence – the feeling of being suspended between worlds, yet rooted in none. Through Tides of Exile, I invite you to join me on this journey of self-discovery and exploration. Each section is a chapter in my own story, but also an invitation for you to bring your own experiences to bear on the poems. Let us navigate these tides together, finding solace in the rhythms that connect us across borders and cultures.
