Brisbane Skies, Stockholm Nights

Parker Wilson
182 haiku
Download PDF Download EPUB
As I sit with my laptop open on a chilly winter morning in Brisbane, I'm transported to a world of contrasts – where sun-kissed streets meet snowflakes softly falling, and ancient medinas whisper secrets to modern cityscapes. This collection, a journey through seasons, landscapes, and languages, is a reflection of my own wanderings as a writer. The haikus within these pages are fragments of memories, like petals unfolding from a flower – delicate, yet resilient. I've drawn inspiration from the places that have shaped me: Marrakech's vibrant markets, Toronto's misty twilight, Casablanca's mysterious nights, and Stockholm's winter wonderland. Technology has woven itself into the fabric of our lives, yet nature remains the constant heartbeat. As I look back on these haikus, I'm struck by the way they've become a kaleidoscope of moments in time – a Technicolor dreamscape where memories blur and blend with the world around me. This collection is an invitation to step into that world, to let go of the present and lose yourself in the whispers of seasons past, and the promise of those yet to come.
