fig split by noon heat —

the pink mouth of a country

I cannot close

the notary stamped

García into the paper—

the sound: a small door

a customer laughs

at something I have not said—

the heat explains it

children in the street

waist-deep in muddy water—

the city becomes sea

the moss is ancient—

older than the name I took,

older than the war

after the spout fades
