Wistful Wanderings

Riley Müller
120 haiku
Download PDF Download EPUB
As I sit with these haiku, gathered like leaves on a forest floor, I'm reminded of the countless moments that have shaped me – the whispers of Brussels' autumn mornings, the London fog's gentle grasp, the rural Indian sun's burning touch, and now, Paris' soft melancholy. Each landscape has etched its own story onto my skin. These haiku are not just snapshots of places; they're fragments of a life lived in transit, where every season becomes a metaphor for growth, loss, and memory. As I've wandered through the years, I've come to realize that nostalgia is not just a bittersweet longing, but also an act of reverence – for the people, landscapes, and moments that have given my life depth. In these pages, I invite you to wander with me through the seasons, where the boundaries between past and present blur, and the beauty of impermanence reveals itself.
