Whispers Of The Wind

Seo Park
193 haiku
Download PDF Download EPUB
As I sit with pen in hand, memories swirl around me like leaves on an autumn breeze. The city streets of Busan, where I grew up, still whisper secrets in my ear – the scent of kimchi from street vendors, the rhythmic calls to prayer from mosques and temples, the distant hum of the port's bustling activity. Yet, as a wanderer at heart, I've also been shaped by the landscapes of New Zealand, its rugged beauty and quiet wisdom seeping into my soul like a gentle rain. In these haikus, I attempt to capture the ephemeral essence of life – the moments when time stands still, and all that's left is the wind's soft whisper. The music of jazz, with its improvisational spirit and longing, has long been a companion on this journey. Its melodies and rhythms have seeped into my bones, influencing the way I see the world and myself within it. These poems are an invitation to step into the spaces where multiple worlds converge – where city streets meet rural landscapes, jazz harmonies intersect with spiritual reflections, and the whispers of the wind carry secrets from one place to another. As you read these haikus, I hope you'll find yourself carried away on the currents of memory and imagination, and that our shared moments will become a bridge between worlds, just as the wind bridges distance.
