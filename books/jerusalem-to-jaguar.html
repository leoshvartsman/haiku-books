Jerusalem To Jaguar

Casey Kowalski
138 haiku
Download PDF Download EPUB
As I sit here in my Mexico City apartment, surrounded by the vibrant sounds and scents of this sprawling metropolis, I am reminded of the winding path that has led me to this moment. Memories from childhood summers spent playing in Jerusalem's ancient streets linger like whispers on the wind, transporting me back to a time before borders and boundaries defined my world. These haiku are fragments of a life lived in flux – a patchwork quilt stitched together from disparate threads. There is the Ann Arbor university campus where I wandered through library stacks, searching for answers; the Guadalajara office cubicle where workdays blurred into nights spent exploring the city's secret corners; and now, Mexico City, with its kaleidoscope of experiences that have shaped me into the person I am today. In these poems, I invite you to walk alongside me through the labyrinthine streets of my mind. We'll weave together moments both joyous and painful, fragments of a life lived in transition. Together, let us surrender to the beauty and the ache, the light and the darkness that has become mine.
