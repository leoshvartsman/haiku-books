Window flower box

Blooms defiant against smog

Beauty claims its space

Mural on brick wall

depicts children who have grown

and moved far away

Church bell counts the hour

over betting shop's neon cross

salvation for sale

Corner store lottery

tickets promise what paychecks

never seem to bring

Bus shelter graffiti

tells tomorrow's history

in aerosol truth

The barista's tattoo

shifts with each espresso pull—

anchor on rough seas
