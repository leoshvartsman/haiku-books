Morning Dusk Awakening

River Lee
191 haiku
Download PDF Download EPUB
As I sit at my desk, surrounded by scribbled notes and half-forgotten tea cups, I am transported back to the countless mornings that have shaped this collection. Morning Dusk Awakening is a distillation of the sensory experiences that have become my life's tapestry – the monsoon rains of Jakarta, the stillness of Kyoto's dawn, the vibrant chaos of Tel Aviv's streets. In these haiku, I've tried to capture the ephemeral beauty of each moment, like grasping at morning mist. The cityscapes, landscapes, and seasons have become intertwined with my own story, as a traveler, a writer, and a seeker. I recall the first light of dawn on a Tel Aviv rooftop, the soft petals of Munich's botanical gardens, and the eerie silence of Kyoto's temple grounds. In weaving these haiku together, I've aimed to create a sense of continuity – between cultures, landscapes, and time itself. It is my hope that this collection will transport you, too, to those fleeting moments when the world seems fresh and full of possibility.
