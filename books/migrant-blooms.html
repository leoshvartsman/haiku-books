Migrant Blooms

Sky Novak

Download PDF Download EPUB
As I wander through the winding streets of my adopted home, Istanbul, I find myself drawn to the fleeting moments that reveal the intricate tapestry of identity and place. Haiku, with its traditional emphasis on simplicity and natural imagery, has become a fitting language for me to explore these themes. My own story – one of migration from the Netherlands to Turkey, of navigating the complexities of belonging in a foreign land – has influenced my perspective as a writer. I've tried to capture the essence of these experiences in Migrant Blooms, this collection of haiku that traverses the boundaries between cultures and cities. From the canals of Amsterdam, where my childhood memories still linger, to the bustling streets of Brussels and the enchanting beauty of Istanbul's Bosphorus, each section represents a thread in the rich weave of my story.
