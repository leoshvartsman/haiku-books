Dawn's Gentle Echoes

Sage Anderson
147 haiku
Download PDF Download EPUB
As I sit on my porch, sipping coffee and watching the sun rise over the hills of Vermont, I am reminded of the threads that weave together to form the tapestry of a life. Memories of childhood summers spent playing in the meadows, of long afternoons spent reading beneath the Stanford oaks, and of solitary walks through Scotland's mist-shrouded glens all blend and swirl in my mind. In this collection, I invite you to join me on a poetic journey through the landscapes that have shaped me. From the stillness of morning to the rhythms of city life, I've sought to capture the essence of each place and the emotions they evoke. Whether it's the golden light of Stanford's campus or the vibrant pulse of Cairo's streets, I've tried to distill the unique character of each location into a few simple words. As you turn these pages, I hope you'll find yourself transported to a world both familiar and unknown. May Dawn's Gentle Echoes resonate within you, reminding you of your own memories, desires, and dreams.
