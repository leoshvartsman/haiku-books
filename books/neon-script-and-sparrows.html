Sample Haiku

Grandmother's fingers

shell fava beans on the stoop—

green pearls in her palm

Stray dog limps past mosque

three legs carrying stubborn hope

through another day

Pigeons nest inside

the old theater's broken sign

where movies once played

Cook chops onions fast,

tears streaming down weathered cheeks—

flavor demands tribute

Street cat claims my lens cap

amber eyes reflecting

old Cairo's shadows

Construction worker

balances rebar on his head

while texting his wife
