River Of Sutras

Camille Robert
153 haiku
Download PDF Download EPUB
As I wandered through Parisian streets, sipping coffee on a misty morning, the soft murmur of incantations from a nearby temple caught my attention. The words blended with the gentle lapping of the Seine against its stone banks, transporting me to a realm where past and present merged. This was just the beginning of my journey, as I discovered that spirituality is not bound by borders or cultures. From Edinburgh's snow-dusted hills to Istanbul's vibrant bazaars, each new destination presented an opportunity to explore the intricate dance between faith, heritage, and landscape. The city streets became a canvas for my observations, with every step and every conversation weaving a tapestry of experiences that would eventually take shape in these haiku. Within this collection, River of Sutras, I invite readers to join me on a poetic journey through the intersections of spirituality, culture, and place. Through the simple yet evocative language of haiku, I aim to capture moments of transcendence, connection, and wonder that arise from embracing diversity and seeking common ground with others.
