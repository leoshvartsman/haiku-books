Islands Of Memory

Casey Kim
128 haiku
Download PDF Download EPUB
As I sit in my Auckland home, surrounded by photographs and trinkets collected from my travels, I am transported to distant lands and memories of a lifetime. The scent of lemongrass wafts through my mind's eye, carrying me back to mornings spent chasing fireflies in Manila's verdant streets. The gentle rustle of oak leaves on Tennessee's campus grounds echoes the quiet moments I spent lost in thought during college days. My journey has taken me far from those sun-kissed mornings and misty springtimes. Nairobi's vibrant markets, with their cacophony of sights and sounds, left an indelible mark on my senses. The city's twilight hours, when shadows stretched long and nights grew cool, were a time for introspection and growth. And now, as I navigate the challenges of the pandemic era, I find myself reaching back to the places that shaped me, seeking solace in memories both fond and bittersweet. In this collection, Islands of Memory, I invite you to join me on a poetic journey through the sensory landscapes of my life. These haiku weave together fragments of experience, evoking the fragrances, textures, and emotions that continue to shape me today.
