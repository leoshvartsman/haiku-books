Winds Of Memory

Ryo Sasaki
151 haiku
Download PDF Download EPUB
As I sit amidst the bustle of Hong Kong's city streets, my mind wanders back to childhood afternoons spent beneath Osaka's cherry blossom trees. The scent of blooming sakura still lingers in my memory, a sweet reminder of home and heritage. It is these fleeting moments that have shaped me, just as the winds of memory carry us through life. This collection, Winds of Memory, is an attempt to capture the essence of those ephemeral experiences – the way Edinburgh raindrops dance on cobblestone streets, the vibrant rhythms of São Paulo's street life, and the majestic skyline of Hong Kong. In weaving together fragments of my past, I hope to create a tapestry that reflects not just personal memories but also the cultural intersections that have made me who I am. As you turn these pages, I invite you to join me on this journey through seasons and cities. Let the haikus transport you to a place where tradition meets modernity, and memories blur into the present. In the words of a wise saying from my childhood: "Kokoro o tsukamu," hold your heart close – for it is there that memories reside.
