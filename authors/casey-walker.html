Casey Walker

2 collections

Casey Walker's work moves between two worlds: the red earth of Outback Australia — spinifex grass, rust-red soil, wide open skies — and the neon hum of a Tokyo apartment. The ancient and the electric. The vast silence of desert and the soft glow of screens.

The work holds a question in the space between those distances: what remains of a landscape when you carry it far from home? No resolution offered. Only the red earth, remembered from a great remove, whispering back.
