Casey Kim

3 collections

Casey Kim's work moves through Accra's markets, Oxford's college chapels at twilight, Copenhagen's cafes, and an Auckland home crowded with photographs and trinkets. The scent of lemongrass. A lifetime collected in objects and afternoons.

The work asks what ordinary moments carry across such distances — what a trinket holds, what a scent returns to. No resolution. Only the accumulation itself, and the question of what it means to keep gathering.
