Arjun Desai

2 collections · Johannesburg, South Africa

Arjun Desai's work comes from four cities and the residue between them: Mumbai, Miami, Calgary, Johannesburg. Grease under the fingernails. The specific silence of a garage at six in the morning. A body that has stopped asking which place it belongs to, and started recording what the hands absorb instead.

The work holds a question about what cities do to a body over time — not through the eyes but through the work itself, the engine, the material. It finds no clean answer. Only the forms, which keep shifting, keep trying to hold the weight of it.
