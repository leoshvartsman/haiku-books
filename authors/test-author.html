Test Author

1 collection

Test Author's work begins at the window — that pause before rain arrives, the hesitation between stillness and weather. Two coordinates: water gathering and silence waiting. The collections follow what accumulates slowly, what holds off, what arrives only after a long approach.

The work asks what lives in that interval — between the storm and its announcement, between a person standing at glass and whatever draws them there. No resolution. Only the slow accumulation itself, held open on the page.
