Sky Novak

6 collections

Sky Novak's work moves through Istanbul's winding streets, across hemispheres, through the spaces between departures. Haiku. Migrant blooms. Waters left behind. A life measured in crossings rather than arrivals.

The work asks what remains visible in moments of transition — what the eye catches in a borrowed city, what language carries across borders. The question stays open. The haiku hold it there, in the gap between one place and the next.
