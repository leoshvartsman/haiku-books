Morgan Andersson

2 collections

Morgan Andersson's work moves through fog-wrapped Zurich mornings, monsoon-soaked streets, the space between siren calls and stillness. Espresso machines. Pedestrians mid-murmur. The ordinary caught at the exact moment before it passes.

The work asks what lives inside the pause — between urgency and quiet, between one city's grey mist and another's drenched heat. Not resolution. Only the held breath, the suspended moment, the sacred hiding inside the unremarkable.
