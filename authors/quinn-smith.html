Quinn Smith

2 collections

Quinn Smith's work begins in the hour before full light — in whispers, in the spaces where one thing becomes another. Two collections. Tapestries and mosaics. The ground between languages, between continents, between the name a child answers to and the name an adult carries.

The work asks what holds when everything is threshold — childhood dissolving into adulthood, leaving folding into arriving, home refusing a fixed definition. No resolution offered. Only the haiku, the quiet moments, the dawn rising four times over, each time from a different coordinate.
