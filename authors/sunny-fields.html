Sunny Fields

1 collection

Sunny Fields works in the space between words — the chambers inside language, the silences that hold the shape of what almost got said. One observation becomes a threshold. One word opens into another, and another after that. The work moves through nested rooms, tracing what language carries inside itself without announcing it.

The question underneath is about containment and excess — what fits inside a word, and what doesn't. Whether silence is absence or its own kind of structure. No resolution offered. Only the next chamber, the next opening, the work continuing to ask what lives inside what.
