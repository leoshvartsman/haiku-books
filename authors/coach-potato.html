Coach Potato

1 collection

Coach Potato's work comes from a specific set of coordinates: the locked door, the shaped couch, the book chosen for an evening that asks nothing. One collection. A particular kind of rest. A life measured in hours that belong only to the self.

The work asks what it means to stop — fully, without apology — and sit with words while the world stays outside. No clean answer. Only the couch, the book, the specific geography of a body at rest, holding the question open.
