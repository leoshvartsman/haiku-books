Carpenter Jones

1 collection

Carpenter Jones works in paint and plaster, in water damage and uneven weight, in the walls we stop seeing because we see them every day. *Rebuilding The House* moves room by room through what accumulates behind surfaces — the record a house keeps of everything that has happened inside it.

The work asks what it means to attend to what has been ignored, to make visible what familiarity has hidden. Every repair opens another layer. The question is never fully answered — only held open, like a wall mid-renovation, exposed and waiting.
