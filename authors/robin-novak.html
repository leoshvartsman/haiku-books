Robin Novak

2 collections · Miami, USA

Robin Novak's work comes from specific coordinates: the ledger columns of exchanged hours, the particular slant of childhood streets, the thin air between altitudes. Miami. The Andes. A life measured in what came in, what went out, what remains. Working-class hours. Bodies that remember elevation.

The work asks what it means to keep your own record — against the records others keep for you. What is owed, what is lost, what the body carries from one altitude to the next. No clean answer. Only the ledger itself, held open.
