Number 10

1 collection

Number 10's work begins where abstraction ends: with the ball, the mud, the net swinging inward. With the body in motion and the crowd's roar and the specific weight of a goal that changes everything. Four collections. A striker. A witness. A life measured in moments of contact between foot and leather.

The work asks what joy actually is when it lives inside a game — not as symbol, not as lesson, but as the thing itself. The beautiful game resists explanation. The collections hold that resistance open, returning again and again to the real.
