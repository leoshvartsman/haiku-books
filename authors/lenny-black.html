Lenny Black

3 collections

Lenny Black's work moves through cul-de-sacs and culverts, vacant lots edged where construction stopped, the hours between lunch and the streetlights coming on. Bikes with loose chains. Suburban ground that is not exotic, not dramatic — just the specific weight of afternoons that stretch too long and end too fast.

The work asks what happens in those in-between hours, in places that were almost something else. Not wilderness, not city — just the familiar, slightly unfinished terrain where kids find the edges of things and wait, without knowing what for.
