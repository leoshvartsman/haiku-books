Valentín García

2 collections

Valentín García's work moves through specific latitudes: the cypress-lined streets of Buenos Aires, the Tennessee hills, the Arabian Gulf, the Parisian glow of streetlights at dusk. Eighty-eight years of standing beneath different skies. A childhood breeze that never quite left. A life assembled from shorelines, river mouths, and meridians.

The work asks what ordinary moments carry across that much distance — laughter in summer streets, the hum of a city at night, the particular light that falls when memory and place overlap. No resolution offered. Only the coordinates themselves, held open.
