Frank Smith

1 collection

Frank Smith's work moves through ballrooms and basements, cardboard floors and parquet, church aisles on Tuesdays and kitchens at midnight. All types of dance. The nervous kind, the sweaty kind, the half-remembered kind done in private when no one is watching.

The work asks what dance actually is when you strip the beautiful away — and refuses to settle. What remains is the full range: exhausted bodies, unlikely rooms, movement that happens anyway. The question stays open on the floor.
