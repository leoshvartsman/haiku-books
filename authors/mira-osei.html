Mira Osei

1 collection

Mira Osei's work begins with the peony — not as symbol, but as fact. The single night. The tearing. The offering made without calculation of cost or duration. One collection. One lifecycle examined until it becomes a way of reading everything else.

The work asks what it means to give completely, knowing the giving cannot last — and does not resolve this into lesson or consolation. Only the image itself remains: a thing that waits, then tears itself open, then is gone.
