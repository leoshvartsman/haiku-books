Avery Johnson

2 collections

Avery Johnson writes from scattered drafts and dog-eared pages, from city streets and the wandering paths between them. *Turbulent Leaves* gathers what accumulates in motion — memories picked up and turned over like something found on a sidewalk. A fusion of places. A life measured in small discoveries.

The work asks what gets noticed when no one is looking for anything in particular — and refuses to tidy the answer. The haiku hold ordinary moments just long enough to feel the weight of them, then let go.
