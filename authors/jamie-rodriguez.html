Jamie Rodriguez

2 collections

Jamie Rodriguez's work begins where most people aren't paying attention: the hour before cities remember themselves, the rustle of leaves underfoot, the window-side dark before the body is asked to be anything. Two collections. The maple and the street below. The fleeting moment held still long enough to become a word.

The work asks what lives inside ordinary thresholds — the seasonal, the pre-dawn, the not-yet. What gets lost when the city wakes, when the leaves settle. No resolution offered. Only the moments themselves, gathered and kept.
