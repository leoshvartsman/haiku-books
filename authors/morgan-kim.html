Morgan Kim

4 collections · Addis Ababa, Ethiopia

Morgan Kim's poems come from a specific set of coordinates: the black loam of Canadian prairies, the red clay of South African townships, the highland earth of Ethiopia. Four collections. A farmer. A wanderer. A life measured in soils.

The work asks what it means to belong to multiple landscapes at once — and finds no clean answer. Only the poems themselves, which hold the question open.
